Holesky Faucet For CSM Testnet

You have to know:

Why claim?
Holesky faucet is provided for some CSM testnet participants who don't have enough ETH/stETH/wstETH as bond.
Who can claim?
Only addresses in the testnet Early Adoption list are eligible to claim.
Which token can I get?
You will be sent any of 3 tokens (i.e. ETH, stETH, or wstETH).
Can I claim in multiple times?
Sorry, each eligible address can claim once. The token amount is enough for you to run at least 1 validator in CSM.
What if I need more Holesky faucet to run multi validators?
Feel free to claim 1 ETH per day on Google Faucet or try PoW Faucet.
Is it developed by Lido DAO?
No, it isn't. It's developed by Lido community members and not affliated with Lido DAO. If you have any issue, please ask in the Lido #csm-testnet discord channel.
